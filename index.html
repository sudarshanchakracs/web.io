Our Services

At Sudarshan Chakra Cyber Security, we are committed to offering comprehensive cybersecurity solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of individuals, businesses, and institutions. Explore our cutting-edge services designed to safeguard your digital assets.

1. Vulnerability Assessments

Identify potential weaknesses in your network, systems, and applications before cybercriminals do. Our detailed vulnerability assessments include:

  • Thorough analysis of network configurations and systems.
  • Identification of outdated software and patches.
  • Customized recommendations to mitigate risks.

2. Penetration Testing

Simulate real-world cyberattacks to uncover vulnerabilities in your infrastructure. Our ethical hacking experts provide:

  • Simulated attacks to test defenses under real-world conditions.
  • Comprehensive reports detailing vulnerabilities and solutions.
  • Post-test consultation to strengthen your defenses.

3. Security Audits

Assess and improve the effectiveness of your existing security policies and measures. Our audits include:

  • Evaluation of compliance with industry standards (ISO, GDPR, etc.).
  • Analysis of existing security protocols and controls.
  • Actionable recommendations for strengthening security posture.

4. Cyber Awareness Training

Empower your team with the knowledge to detect and prevent cyber threats. Our training programs offer:

  • Workshops on phishing detection and secure browsing.
  • Guidelines for safe password practices and multi-factor authentication.
  • Regular updates on emerging cyber threats.

5. Incident Response and Recovery

Minimize damage and restore normalcy in the event of a cyber incident. Our services include:

  • Rapid response to security breaches.
  • Data recovery and forensic analysis to determine the source of the attack.
  • Development of incident management plans to prevent future occurrences.

6. Managed Security Services

Leave your cybersecurity management to the experts. Our managed services include:

  • 24/7 monitoring of your IT environment for threats.
  • Regular updates and patches to maintain system integrity.
  • Customized dashboards for real-time security insights.
